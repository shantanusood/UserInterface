Trade initiation strategies
 > No new trades when less than 30 DTE
 > Start next month when less than 60 DTE
 > Only trade high volume securities
 > Stay at least 1 SD away, and greater than 70% POP

Trade managment/termination strategies
 > Close all trades with less than 3-5% short strike to ITM by 7 DTE
 > If short strike less than 1-2% to ITM then close the trade
 > If profit reaches 60% start to close the trade
 > If still greater than 25-30 DTE and trade is less than 1-2% to ITM, adjust by buying 
   another trade with premium equal to greater than the loss on this trade
